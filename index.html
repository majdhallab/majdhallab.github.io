Background

I've spent 10+ years building programs that actually work, in one of the most complex humanitarian environments in the world. Key initiatives

Work that makes a difference.

Emergency Food Response, Tripoli

Rapid food assistance to over 30,000 displaced people across multiple crises, including the war on Lebanon, displacement from Syria, and recurring emergencies affecting both host and displaced communities.

School Feeding Expansion Program

Scaling daily meals to over 80,000 children in underserved public schools across North Lebanon and Akkar, ensuring consistent nutrition for the most vulnerable students every school day.

Community Kitchen Support Initiative

Developed, set up, and operationalized a series of community kitchens dispersed across Tripoli, each feeding up to 2,000 people per day, providing hot meals and creating sustainable community food systems.

Winterization and Shelter Support

Providing essential winter items and shelter support to over 5,000 families affected by harsh seasonal conditions, ensuring basic protection and dignity during the most difficult months of the year.

Local Capacity Strengthening Program

Assessing the needs of local grassroots organizations and delivering targeted capacity building through on-the-job coaching, helping partners build accountable and sustainable humanitarian response systems.

Multi-Sector Business Ventures

Leading and managing ventures across construction, food services, and local businesses in Lebanon, including Warshati Engineering Solutions, a private interior design and rehabilitation company.
